About

Ozaki Moving Corp has a recent history of service based on soundness and honesty, always following it’s original slogan:

“Your move is our business.”

In this period, Ozaki Moving Corp has met it’s clients and suppliers’ requirements with responsibility and efficiency, and thanks to this interaction, it’s becoming a reliable and fair company with a permanent growth. In the moment that you hire our services you will expect a crew fully-trained, uniformed, and concerned about your needs, that we are sure will reach.

Ozaki’s administrative and technical team is constantly updated in the globalized world; it is a team committed to give an efficient and opportune service upon request. Ozaki Moving has it’s own trucks fleet and wide operation and storage facilities.
